MITIKU SHONTO COFFEE EXPORT

Our Story

Mitiku Shonto Coffee Export company, founded in 2009, is a leading coffee bean producer in Ethiopia. With a workforce of over 522 employees, we specialize in the production of high-quality coffee beans. Our annual production reaches approximately 743,750 bags. Alongside our main processing facility, we have dedicated sites for dry coffee processing and washing station operations.
