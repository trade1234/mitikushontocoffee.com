About Us

MITIKU SHONTO COFFEE EXPORT

Our Story

Mitiku Shonto, a visionary entrepreneur with a deep appreciation for coffee, founded our company in 2009. His expertise in the industry and dedication to quality laid the foundation for our success.Since then, we have grown into one of the premier coffee producers in the region, known for our commitment to excellence and sustainable practices.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become one of the reputed and honored Ethiopian coffee producers and Exporter, which will work to words the branding of Ethiopian coffee worldwide and spread and export the best quality coffee to the global market

Our Mission

Our mission is to export different types of coffees and be good competitor by coffee industry.

WASHING STATION DETAILS

LOCATION: KOCHERE DISTRICT

Further enhancing our coffee production capabilities,we have a dedicated washing station in the Kochere district. Here, we carefully wash and process the coffee cherries, preserving the unique flavors and aromas that make Ethiopian coffee so renowned.

DRY COFFEE PROCESSING AND COMBINING SITE

LOCATION: YIRGACHEFFE CITY, 02 KEBELE

In addition to our main processing facility, we have a specialized site for dry coffee processing and combining. Located in the beautiful 02 Kebele of Yirgacheffe city, this site is equipped with state-of-the-art machinery to ensure the highest quality coffee beans.
